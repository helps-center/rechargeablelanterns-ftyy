Privacy Policy

1. Intro

Your privacy is super important to us. This Privacy Policy tells you how we handle your info when you're on our job board.

2. What Info We Grab

We might collect this stuff from job hunters and employers:

For Job Hunters:

*   Your name, email, phone number, and where you are.
*   Your resume and cover letters.
*   Work history, what you studied, and what you're good at.
*   The kind of jobs you want and your saved searches.
*   Jobs you've applied to and messages you've sent.

For Employers:

*   Your company's name, your name, email, and what jobs you're listing.
*   Payment info (if you're paying for fancy job listings).
*   Messages you've sent to candidates.

For Everyone:

*   Login info and how you set up your account.
*   Info about your device (like its IP address, browser, etc.).
*   What you do on our site (like jobs you view, applications, etc.).

3. How We Put Your Info to Work

We use your info to:

*   Make and manage user accounts.
*   Help job seekers find and apply for jobs.
*   Help employers post jobs and search for people.
*   Let job seekers and employers talk to each other.
*   Make our platform better and easier to use.
*   Process payments for special features (if you use them).
*   Send you email alerts about jobs (only if you want them).
*   Stop spam, scams, and people using the site when they shouldn't.

4. Who Sees Your Resume

*   Employers who've signed up might be able to see your resume.
*   You can choose if you want your profile to be private, public, or only searchable if you say it's okay.
*   You can change these settings in your account whenever you want.

5. Other Companies We Use

*   We might share your info with companies we trust for things like:
    *   Hosting our site (like AWS, Google Cloud)
    *   Handling payments (like Stripe, Razorpay)
    *   Sending emails
    *   Analytics (like Google Analytics)
    *   Checking backgrounds (only if you say it's okay)

These companies only get the info they need to do their jobs.

6. How Long We Keep Your Info

We keep your info as long as your account is active or as long as the law says we have to. You can delete your account anytime, and we'll get rid of your info unless we have to keep it for legal reasons.

7. What You Can Do

You have the right to:

*   See your personal info.
*   Change your info.
*   Control who sees your resume or contact info.
*   Delete your account and ask us to remove your info.
*   Stop getting promotional emails.

8. Cookies

We might use cookies to:

*   Keep you logged in.
*   Remember what kind of jobs you're searching for.
*   Track how people use our site so we can make it better and keep it secure.

You can manage cookies in your browser settings.

9. Security

We try our best to keep your info safe. But no system is perfect, so use strong passwords and keep an eye on your account.

10. Kids' Privacy

This platform is not for kids under 16. We don't knowingly collect info from them.

11. Policy Updates

We might update this Privacy Policy every now and then. If we do, we'll post the changes on this page.
